About Me

I remember getting AOL for the family computer when I was 8 or 9. The internet is a technology that imprints its importance even on a young child. Growing up using the internet to research hobbies (including distance-running, playing guitar and gaming), the internet grew to become an education tool, a news-source, a font of entertainment, an in-home commerce network, a social space and even more -- not just for me, but also the greater world around me.

After studying Criminal Justice and Philosophy, I worked in accounting roles for the better part of a decade after graduating from Indiana University. I was looking for something else out of my work-life, and the challenges that arose around the Covid-19 pandemic proved to be a catalyst. I enrolled at Eleven-Fifty Academy in Indianapolis, to study Full-Stack Web Development. I look forward to learning more, and contributing to the internet as it continues to grow.

Please see some of my work below. I hope to wow you with something -- if not now, in the future.
